Brazil







Brazil has rich culture and its people are one of a kind. Its located in the eastern part of South America and has tropical climates. Once a year its host to a celebration through out the city of Rio De Jenero called Carnival which is similar to New Orleans' Mardi Gras. Some of the cities in Brazil are:

  1. Rio de Jenero
  2. Espirito Santo
  3. Paraiba
  4. Sao Paulo
